We need a new solution for new realities.

As climate change and population growth continue, disasters strike faster and chronic stresses last even longer.

Over the last 30 years, one-third of spending on development has been lost to recurrent crises, totaling 3.8 trillion dollars worldwide.

The Global Resilience Partnership is building systemic approaches to addressing climate and population change.

The Global Resilience Challenge is a multi-phase design challenge, focused on bringing together people and organizations from across sectors to collaborate on bold and innovative solutions to the toughest resilience challenges.
